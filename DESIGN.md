## Style Prompt

Dark premium product launch identity: a near-black green canvas, luminous teal system light, and a restrained warm-metal accent. The product should feel precise, quiet, and engineered rather than flashy. Use generous negative space, one strong title moment, thin interface details, and slow cinematic motion over abstract background footage.

## Colors

- Background: `#07100F` deep green-black
- Foreground: `#F2F0E8` soft ivory
- Muted text: `#A8B8B4` cool silver
- Primary accent: `#2DF2C8` luminous teal
- Secondary accent: `#F4D06F` muted champagne

## Typography

- Display: `Fraunces`, 800 weight, tight launch-title voice
- Interface/body: `Space Grotesk`, 300-600 weights, precise product UI voice

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use blue-purple neon gradients.
- Do not center every element with equal visual weight.
- Do not use generic pure black or pure white.
- Do not add card stacks or nested card layouts.
- Do not use busy explanatory text; the first frame must feel like the product intro itself.
